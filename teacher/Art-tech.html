Art and Technology

Overall Curriculum

Perry Hall High School, Baltimore MD

Course Overview

This course will provide opportunities for students to understand ways that art and design concepts can be used for visual communication and presentation. Students will learn the basics of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator as well as studying principles for designing with type. They will have the opportunity to create computer drawings, animations, personal calling cards, and iron-on transfers.

This is a half year course designed to:

  • Introduce students to computer graphics and art software
  • Explore various applications in real life for these software applications
  • Encourage the development of individual artistic preferences

Edmodo

During class we will be using an online program called EDMODO to turn in assignments and post resources.

Lessons

Digital Collage

Create a digital collage of a person constructed out of at least 6 body parts.

This collage must: Be of a person or a metamorphosis of a person and animal/machine/character (meaning you may use just body parts of a person, or combine a person with something else, still using at least 6 parts)

Selective and Additive Color

Using Stock Photos, create five selective color photographs (image is black and white except for one area which remains a color) and five additive color photographs (take a black and white image and hand color over it).

Multiple Self-Portrait

Create a Narritive Self-Portrait that uses multiple photos of yourself.

Self-Portriat: Lines and Shapes

Using a photo of yourself, draw a contour line portrait self-portrait. Use the line portrait to create shapes of values in different color schemes.

Warhol Pop Art

Useing a stock photo create a pop art style image in four different colors.

Movie Poster

Use Photoshop to create an advertising poster for a movie of your choice.

Introduction to Illustrator

Learn to use the Pen tool to create lines and shapes.

Logos

Use Illustrator to create a personal business card with a logo that represents you.

CD Cover

Use Photoshp or Illustrator to create a CD cover package. Create the cover, back, and insert for a musician.

T-Shirt Design

Create a design that will be printed out and ironed on to a t-shirt.

Photoshop Animation

Create an animation using Adobe Photoshop.
