Art and Technology

Overall Curriculum

Perry Hall High School, Baltimore MD

Course Overview

This course will provide opportunities for students to understand ways that art and design concepts can be used for visual communication and presentation. Students will learn the basics of Adobe Photoshop and Illustrator as well as studying principles for designing with type. They will have the opportunity to create computer drawings, animations, personal calling cards, and iron-on transfers.

This is a half year course designed to:

  • Introduce students to computer graphics and art software
  • Explore various applications in real life for these software applications
  • Encourage the development of individual artistic preferences
